## Why test this

Most retirement calculators ask 3 questions and give you a chart. This one is different in two ways:

- **Real math.** Full year-by-year projection — taxes (fed + state + IRMAA), RMDs, Social Security, Roth conversions, withdrawal sequencing. Same engine professionals use.
- **An AI advisor that uses your plan.** Not a chatbot. Ask *"Am I on track to retire at 62?"* and it runs the projection. Ask *"Should I do a Roth conversion?"* and it models a 25-year ladder. Click **⚡ Optimize** and it returns a ranked action list with dollar impact per change.

---

## Three things to do

1. **Set up your plan** — *My Plan* tab, ~5 min. Real numbers, approximate is fine.
2. **Open the AI Advisor tab** and click **⚡ Optimize**. Watch it chain projection → SS timing → Roth ladder → withdrawal order analysis in ~20 seconds.
3. **Chat with the advisor**. Try: *"Am I on track?"*, *"When should I claim Social Security?"*, *"What's my biggest risk?"*
